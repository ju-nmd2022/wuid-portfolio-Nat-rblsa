ento: Case Study // Rebranding

Context

To bring to the Latin American context the case of the Ento Project, in which proposes the replacement of animal protein intake from livestock by protein from insects, as a global feeding strategy for the coming years. Global demand for food is accelerating and the areas of agriculture and livestock cannot keep up. The current sources of protein have a huge environmental impact. The livestock industry is 70% of the agricultural land.

Requirements

Provide a sense of familiarity with another dish or food.

Do not show the insects in the food.

No need much preparation or time to cook.

Have a reference about the taste of the insects.

Know where these insects come from and preferably by a sustainable company.

Proposal

With the packaging redesign proposal we want to be able to visaully atract the consumer to achieve a first approach with curiosity and then with a physical interaction with the design of the products. The design would describe in a better way the content of the product and its benefits, but we also seek to create a packaging that the user wants to have and try.

Channels

It would be sold online on the official website of the product and in physical stores, not stores such as supermarkets, but stores that seek to be more naturist and organic since that is where our target users go, so it will gain popularity until it is required by conventional stores.
